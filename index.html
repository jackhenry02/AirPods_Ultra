    • Conversation Awareness.

      AirPods Pro 3 can detect when you’re talking to someone nearby and automatically lower the volume of whatever you’re listening to. When you’re done, the volume goes right back to where you had it. 17

    Audio performance

    The sound of science.

    Powered by the Apple-designed H2 chip, AirPods Pro 3 feature a completely redesigned acoustic architecture that brings breathtaking, all-around-you audio to everything you listen to — from your pump-up playlist to your weekly FaceTime call with family.

    A new multiport acoustic architecture with more precise airflow control — featuring an inward-facing microphone that points directly into the ear canal — delivers deeper bass, vivid vocal clarity and a wider soundstage, so you never miss a note.

    A custom-built driver and amplifier work with the H2 chip to create high-definition, three‑dimensional sound, with superlow distortion during playback.

    Personalised listening

    For your ears only.

    AirPods Pro 3 feature next-generation Adaptive EQ, which customises a sound signature for your unique ear geometry and fit. And Personalised Volume uses machine learning to understand your listening patterns and adapt to your preferences over time. 18

    Adaptive EQ tunes music to your ears in real time based on the fit of your AirPods. Inward-facing microphones measure what you’re hearing, then adjust the low to high frequencies of a song — so you get consistently detailed playback, every time.

    Personalised Spatial Audio uses the True­Depth camera on your iPhone to create a custom profile based on the geometry of your head, delivering deeply immersive sound tailored to you.

    Dynamic head tracking makes conversations feel like they’re happening in the same room — whether you’re gaming with friends or hosting a Group FaceTime call with family. 19

    Fitness

    Introducing heart rate sensing.

    All-new heart rate sensing helps you track your heart rate and calories burned during workouts. 3 Using LEDs that pulse invisible light 256 times per second — and sensor fusion from the accelerometers — AirPods Pro 3 give you incredibly accurate data for up to 50 different workout types in the Fitness app. And with a more secure in-ear fit, you can go all out and they won’t come out.

    Fitness app. With just AirPods Pro 3 and iPhone, you can dive into the new workout experience in the Fitness app, which includes things like walking, cycling and HIIT. You can set your favourite music and podcasts to play automatically when you start a workout. With the new Apple Intelligence–enabled Workout Buddy feature, you can get personalised messages that keep you going. 20 And with access to your Move ring — and even the Burn Bar in Fitness+ — you might just never stop.

    IP57 dust, sweat and water resistance.


    A first for AirPods, an IP57 rating means AirPods Pro 3 are more durable than ever. 10 Thanks to precision engineering combined with robust testing, you can feel confident during even the sweatiest workouts.

    • Made with 40% recycled material by weight.

      Recycled Material

      Our approach.

      Using recycled materials, like 65% recycled plastic in the case, reduces the need to mine new material, which avoids the carbon emissions and environmental impacts of mining.

       Learn more in our Product Environmental Report (PDF) (opens in new window)

      How you can help.

      You can protect the earth’s precious resources by trading in, passing on or recycling devices and accessories you no longer use. You can bring them into any Apple Store for free, secure recycling. We also offer other ways to recycle, including options by post.

       Learn how to trade in or recycle devices

    • Manufactured with 40% renewable electricity.

      Renewable Electricity

      Our approach.

      Using electricity from renewable sources like wind and solar across our global supply chain — instead of fossil fuels — significantly reduces carbon emissions from manufacturing Apple products.

       Learn more in our Product Environmental Report (PDF) (opens in new window)

      How you can help.

      You can extend the lifespan of your batteries and Apple products by using Optimised Battery Charging. It works by learning your charging habits and delaying the final charge — from 80% to 100% — until you are likely to need it.

       Learn more about Optimised Battery Charging

    • Delivered in compact packaging for 25% more units per trip.

      Packaging and Shipping

      Our approach.

      We designed the AirPods Pro 3 box to be smaller than previous boxes, which reduces carbon emissions from transportation because it weighs less and more units can be delivered per trip.

       Learn more in our Product Environmental Report (PDF) (opens in new window)

      How you can help.

      New Apple products come in paper packaging that’s 100% fibre-based. In most places, you can put the entire box into your household recycling bin. You can also bring Apple packaging to any Apple Store and we’ll recycle it for free.
